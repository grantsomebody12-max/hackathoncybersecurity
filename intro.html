Introduction to Cybersecurity

The Digital Fortress

Imagine your digital life as a house. You have doors, windows, and a safe where you keep your most valuable possessions. Cybersecurity acts as the locks, alarms, and security guards that protect your digital house from intruders.

Why Does It Matter?

Every time you connect to the internet, you are exposed to potential threats. Hackers, scammers, and malicious software are constantly looking for open windows or weak locks to steal your personal information, money, or identity.

Your Journey Begins Here

In this learning path, you will discover how to build an impenetrable fortress around your digital life. You don't need to be a coding expert—just aware and proactive.
