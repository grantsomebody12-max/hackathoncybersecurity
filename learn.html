Learn Cybersecurity Basics

Phishing

Fake emails and websites designed to steal your information. Always check the sender's address and be wary of links that ask for your login credentials or personal data.

Malware

Malicious software that can spy on or damage your device. This includes viruses, spyware, and ransomware. Avoid downloading files from untrusted sources.

Passwords

Strong passwords are your first line of defense. Use a mix of upper/lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Consider a password manager to create and store unique, complex passwords for every site.
